## üéØ PART 10: COMMON QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

### Q: Do I have to wear my smartwatch?
**A:** Yes! It automatically sends your heart rate and oxygen to the app every 15 minutes. Just wear it like a regular watch.

### Q: How often should I add manual vitals?
**A:** 
- Blood Pressure: **Once a day** (or as your doctor says)
- Temperature: **Only if sick**
- Weight: **Once a week** (same time each day)
- Blood Sugar: **If diabetic, as directed by doctor**

### Q: What if I don't have a blood pressure machine?
**A:** You can still use the app! You'll see smartwatch vitals (HR, SpO2). Ask your doctor about getting a home blood pressure machine.

### Q: Can my caregivers see everything I do?
**A:** No, they only see:
- Your health vitals
- Your location (if you enable it)
- Medicine reminders (if you're taking them)
- Emergency alerts

They can't see: your messages, calls, or personal apps.

### Q: What if the app crashes?
**A:** 
1. Close the app completely
2. Reopen it
3. Your data is already saved (don't worry!)
4. Try again

If it keeps crashing:
- Restart your phone
- Reinstall the app
- Contact SafeNest support

### Q: Is my health information safe?
**A:** Yes! SafeNest uses:
- üîí Encrypted database (like a bank)
- üîê Password protection
- üõ°Ô∏è Only your caregivers can see data
- üìµ Your location is private

### Q: What if I accidentally press SOS?
**A:** Don't worry!
- You have **10 seconds** to cancel
- Press the **X** or **Cancel** button
- Alert won't be sent
- It's completely normal

### Q: Can I call my caregiver from the app?
**A:** Yes!
1. Tap üë• **Caregivers**
2. Find the caregiver
3. Tap **Call** button
4. Your regular phone call connects
5. Talk normally

### Q: What if I forget to add a vital reading?
**A:** It's okay! You can:
- Add it later (tap the time to adjust)
- Skip it if you don't remember exact number
- Your caregiver will remind you if needed
